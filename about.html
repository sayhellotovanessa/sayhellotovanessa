Hello. I am Vanessa Guedes, a front-end developer with some background in education and project management.

Originally from Brazil, currently I work at Britny, in Stockholm, Sweden. I am sure they hired me because I told them that I am a Linux fangirl. And that is true.

A passionate by technology and people.

I work with IT because I believe that sharing knowledge and distributing information are powerful things. And making it accessible to everybody is the developer's responsability.

Some skills would be good to show off.

I love CSS animations and I can spend many hours working on strange ideas about drawing on the screen via code. Of course I have some experience with JavaScript, speacially JQuery, but I must say that vanilla js makes me a little bit happier. I also like the shell - not the ones in the sea, but that dark and dull command line interpreter - and have my own CLI aliases. When working, I use Atom as my code editor and some Linux distribution as my OS (it always depends on the last releases and how much interested I am in some new distro). I have worked with Wordpress and PHP stuff for many years, but now I am trying to focus on other type of projects. I am very interested in Python and Golang.
