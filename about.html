I am a Brazilian developer who lives in Sweden. Currently working at Britny, in Stockholm - where I live with my cats, my books and my partner. I work mostly as a front-end but also have some experience with project management. There are many things that I like in the IT area but above all them is the possibility to make people's lives easier. That is why I work as a web developer. I believe that the information should be acessible for everybody in the whole world. My work is mostly based on using open source projects, frameworks and languages.

I appreciate working with people who truly believe in what they are doing with their time every day, not only thinking that 'time is money'. Because time is your life. I like to apply some of the Hacker Ethics to my carrer as well:

  • Sharing
  • Openess
  • Decentralization
  • World improvement
